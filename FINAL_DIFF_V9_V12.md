# Difference between your old uploaded build (v9) and the new final product build (v12)

## Old uploaded build (v9)
- Strong internal company tool
- Better UI than early versions
- Good split between operations and email work
- Campaign / profile / tracking foundation existed
- Still felt like an internal system more than a reusable product
- Auto email logic was not yet complete
- No proper reply / bounce / unsubscribe control layer
- No sequence engine for multi-step follow-up

## New final build (v12)
- Clearer product identity: **TradeFlow CRM — Altahhan Edition**
- Intro page, tour, cleaner product framing, stronger management-facing UX
- Unified outreach workspace with:
  - Campaigns
  - Sequences
  - Replies & Suppression
  - Profiles
  - Templates
  - Logs
  - Tracking
  - Bridge tools
- New **auto email sequences** with multiple steps and scheduling logic
- **Stop-on-reply** logic so follow-up can stop when the customer answers
- **Suppression list** so bounced or unsubscribed emails are blocked from future outreach
- **Manual inbox event logging** for replies, bounces, and unsubscribes
- **Public unsubscribe link** embedded into outreach emails
- Better outreach search visibility from the unified workspace search
- Stronger bilingual navigation and product language for the new modules

## Real business difference
The old build looked like:
> a strong internal tool

The new final build looks more like:
> a reusable CRM + outreach product that can be sold, branded, and expanded for other companies later

## Biggest commercial improvement
The biggest upgrade is not color or layout.
It is that the outreach area now behaves more like a product engine:
- send once with campaigns
- follow up automatically with sequences
- stop safely when the contact replies
- suppress bad or unsubscribed addresses
