# Al Tahhan Export Tool - Product Summary

## Core workspaces
1. **Operations**: dashboard, shipments, follow-ups, tasks, documents, invoices, packing lists, clients.
2. **Email Tool**: campaigns, profiles, templates, logs, Outlook Bridge, bridge monitor, tracking.

## Why this structure is better
- Keeps export operations separate from email work.
- Reduces the long sidebar problem.
- Makes the product easier for staff to learn.
- Keeps Al Tahhan branding visible across the whole system.

## Main value
- Shipment tracking with ETA / ETD and missing documents.
- Follow-up and reminders for export work.
- Manual or PDF/image entry for invoice and packing list.
- Branded email sending workflow for each employee.
- Access to details from outside the office.
